About SugarCafeCharms

SugarCafeCharms is a cozy little clay studio run by a quiet creator in India, crafting handmade trinkets filled with love, pastels, and a sprinkle of magic. Each charm tells a story — soft, cutesy, and uniquely yours. Whether it’s a tiny bunny, a dreamy milk carton, or your custom fantasy idea, we’re here to bring it to life, one charm at a time.
